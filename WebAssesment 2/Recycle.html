Recycling in Otago

There are two different bins at home used for personal recycling. A yellow bin and a blue bin, below you can see what can and cant go in each bin.

Recycle!

These items do go in the blue bin!

  • Beverage Bottle
  • Food Bottles
  • Jars

Yellow Bins

  • Plastic Bottles and Containers
  • Cardboard Boxes
  • Aluminium Tins and Cans
  • Books and News papers

Remember to!

  • Rinse out jars, containers and tins
  • Flatten Cardboard
  • Keep lids on bottles
  • Remove covers off of books

Dont Recycle!

The blue bin cant take these

  • Broken Glass
  • Windows
  • Mirrors
  • Cups and other glassware

Yellow bins dont take

  • Electronics
  • Plastic Bags
  • Batteries
  • Metal
  • Glass
  • Clothing
  • Polystyrene or milk cartons
  • Medical Waste

Dont!

  • Bend or crush cans
  • Leave food in jars or containers
  • Seperate caps from bottles
  • Bundle recycleables in plastics bags

What do i do with my medical waste?

Any sryngies or medication can be handed into you local pharmacy where it can be properly disposed of.

How do i dispose of my electronics?

Electronics can only be recyled at landfills equiped to deal with electronic waste. Consult our page on E-waste for more information
