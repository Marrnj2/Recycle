How to recycle

Home Recycling

Along with normal trash collection hired by your local council Recycling collecting is contracted also. This service is coverd by your rates. There are two bins that can be used to recycle different forms of waste.

Blue Bins

  • Jars
  • Bottles
  • Glassware
  • Broken Glass
  • Mirrors
  • Sheet Glass

Yellow Bins

Yellow Bin recycleing

Recycleable
  • Hard Plastic
  • Cardboard
  • Aluminium Cans
  • Tins
  • Paper
Unrecycleable
  • Food Containers
  • Soft Plastic
  • Disposeable cutlury
  • Electronics
  • Metal

Public Recycling

Bins

Around Otago you will find bins that allow you to recycle your plastic and glass. These bins are not for disposeing of recycle from your house, just for what you pick up while on the go

Bottle Banks

Large bottle banks are avalible. These can be used if you have a mountan of beer bottles overflowing from your blue bins.
