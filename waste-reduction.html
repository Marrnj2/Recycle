Reduceing your impact on the enviroment

Reuse

Op shops

Donating to op shops is a great to get rid of items you dont want without harming the enviroment. So many things can be donated to op shops like clothes, crokary, glass ware and so many others. Another great thing about op shops is you can always find great things while shopping here you cant find any where else.

Rummage Day

Some landfills like the Green Island Landfill in Dunedin allow people to drop off items in good condition free of charge . They then let others come in an "Rummage" through the items. Just like OP shopping you can find many different things here ranging from books a beds

Transport

Instead of driving to work conisder other options. Carpooling is a greatway to reduce the number of cars on the road and gas being consumed. Or why not try bikeing to get around, save the enviroment and your body!

Buy Localy

Farmers markets are a great way to reduce your impact the enviroment. Small farms have less of an impact on the enviroment. Locally produced food does not have to travel as far as imported produce saving emmisons made by transporting the goods
